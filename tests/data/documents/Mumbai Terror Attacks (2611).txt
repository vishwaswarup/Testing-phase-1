Mumbai Terror Attacks (26/11)

Date: 26 November 2008

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra

Attackers:
Lashkar-e-Taiba militants

Targets:
Taj Mahal Palace Hotel
Oberoi Trident Hotel
Nariman House
Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus

Fatalities:
166

Injuries:
More than 300

Summary:
A coordinated series of terrorist attacks was carried out across Mumbai over four days. Attackers arrived by sea and targeted multiple locations.
